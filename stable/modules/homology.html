gtda.homology: Persistent homology¶

The module gtda.homology implements transformers to generate persistence diagrams.

homology.VietorisRipsPersistence([metric, …])

Persistence diagrams resulting from Vietoris-Rips filtrations.

homology.SparseRipsPersistence([metric, …])

Persistence diagrams resulting from Sparse Vietoris-Rips filtrations.

homology.EuclideanCechPersistence([…])

Persistence diagrams resulting from `Cech filtrations <>`_.

homology.CubicalPersistence([…])

Persistence diagrams resulting from filtered cubical complexes.
