ForgetDimension¶

class gtda.diagrams.ForgetDimension[source]¶

Replaces all homology dimensions in persistence diagrams with numpy.inf.

Useful when downstream tasks require the use of topological features all at once – and not separated between different homology dimensions.

See also

gtda.homology.VietorisRipsPersistence
