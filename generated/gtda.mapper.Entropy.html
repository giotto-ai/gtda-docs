gtda.mapper.Entropy¶

class gtda.mapper.Entropy[source]¶

Entropy of rows in a two-dimensional array.

The rows of the array are interpreted as probability vectors, after taking absolute values if necessary and normalizing. Then, their Shannon entropies are computed and returned.
